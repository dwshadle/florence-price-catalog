## Alternate Title
- Symphony in E Minor
- Symphony No. 3 in E Minor[^fn1]

## Composition Year
- 1931&ndash;1932

## Instrumentation
- 2+2pic.2.2.2 - 4.2.3.1 - timp.3perc - strings

## Length
- 40 min.
